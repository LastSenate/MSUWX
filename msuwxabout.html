We are (Mostly) various engineering students attending Morehead State University. We started this club as a way to assist our communities in Eastern Kentucky facing severe weather and natural disasters.

Although we are majority engineers that do research and development for the club, the majority of what we do is community service in form of drives, warnings, and this website itself.
